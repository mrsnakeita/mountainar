📋 Introduction

Mountain AR is an augmented reality app that identifies mountains through your device's camera. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

We believe in full transparency: Mountain AR does not collect, store or sell any personal data.

📡 Data We Use

📍 Location (GPS)

Used to find mountains near you. Processed in real time on your device only — never saved or sent to any server.

📷 Camera

Used to display the AR overlay. The video stream is never recorded, saved or transmitted.

🧭 Compass & Motion Sensors

Used to detect the direction your device is facing and position AR labels correctly. Never saved or transmitted.

💳 Purchase Data (Pro only)

In-app purchase confirmation is handled entirely by Apple or Google. We never receive or store any payment or financial information.

What we do NOT collect

Personal information Email or name Analytics Advertising ID Photos or videos Browsing history Cookies Tracking
