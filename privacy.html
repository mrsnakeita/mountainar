# Privacy Policy – Mountain AR **Last updated:** April 2025 **Developer:** [Your name / company name] **Contact:** [your@email.com] --- ## 1. Introduction Mountain AR ("the app") is an augmented reality application that allows users to identify mountains through their device's camera. This Privacy Policy describes what data is collected, how it is used, and what rights you have as a user. --- ## 2. Data Collected ### 2.1 Location Data (GPS) - **What we collect:** the geographic position of the device (latitude and longitude) - **Why:** to calculate the distance and direction of nearby mountains - **How:** only during active use of the app, never in the background - **Retention:** location data is never saved or transmitted to third parties. It is used exclusively in real time on the device ### 2.2 Camera - **What we collect:** the video stream from the rear camera - **Why:** to display augmented reality on the screen - **How:** the video stream is never recorded, saved or transmitted. It is displayed only in real time on the device ### 2.3 Compass and Motion Sensors - **What we collect:** device orientation (compass, gyroscope) - **Why:** to calculate the direction the user is facing and position AR labels accordingly - **Retention:** sensor data is never saved or transmitted ### 2.4 Purchase Data (Pro version only) - **What we collect:** confirmation of in-app purchase via Apple App Store or Google Play Store - **Why:** to unlock Pro features - **Note:** payment data is handled exclusively by Apple or Google. The developer has no access to any financial information of the user ### 2.5 Data We Do Not Collect The app does **not** collect or transmit: - Name, email or other personally identifiable information - Demographic data - Usage data or analytics - Advertising identifiers (IDFA/GAID) - Browsing history - Camera content (photos or videos) --- ## 3. Third-Party Services The app uses the following external services to function: ### 3.1 OpenStreetMap / Overpass API - **Purpose:** download mountain peak data near the user's location - **Data transmitted:** approximate GPS coordinates (search radius) - **Privacy policy:** https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy ### 3.2 Open-Meteo - **Purpose:** retrieve weather forecasts for mountain peaks (Pro version only) - **Data transmitted:** GPS coordinates of the selected peak - **Privacy policy:** https://open-meteo.com/en/terms - **Note:** Open-Meteo does not require registration and does not collect personal data ### 3.3 Apple App Store / Google Play Store - **Purpose:** in-app purchase management - **Data transmitted:** handled directly by Apple/Google according to their policies - **Apple Privacy Policy:** https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ - **Google Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy --- ## 4.
